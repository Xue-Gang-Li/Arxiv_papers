Timelike Quantum Energy Teleportation

Kazuki Ikeda
摘要：We establish a novel quantum protocol called Timelike Quantum Energy Teleportation (TQET), designed for transporting quantum energy across space-time. This protocol uses temporal and spatial quantum correlations between agents separated by space and time. The energy supplier injects energy into the system by measuring the ground state of a many-body system that evolves over time, while the distant recipient performs a conditional operation using feedback from the supplier. When both supplier and recipient operate within the same time frame, this is called Quantum Energy Teleportation (QET). A proof-of-concept was performed for the Ising model, utilizing quantum simulations. TQET increases energy efficiency from approximately 3% to around 40%, representing over a 13-fold improvement compared to QET. Furthermore, we analyzed the relationship between entanglement in time and TQET, validating the role of temporal correlations in energy activation between agents across space-time.

                                  https://arxiv.org/abs/2412.20812

                                    Tunable pairing with local spin-dependent Rydberg molecule potentials in an atomic Fermi superfluid

                                    Chih-Chun Chien, Seth T. Rittenhouse, S. I. Mistakidis, H. R. Sadeghpour
                                    摘要：我们探索了两组件原子费米超流体在可调节配对相互作用存在下的能谱和本征态，其中包括自旋依赖的超长距离 Rydberg 分子势。我们利用 Bogoliubov-de Gennes 形式进行研究。吸引性的 ULRM 势导致局部密度积累，而它们之间的差异产生了一个局部极化势，并诱导出能量低于大规模能隙的间隙中的 Yu-Shiba-Rusinov (YSR) 状态。当配对强度降至临界值以下时，从均匀分布到人口失衡的转变发生，同时出现具有相位相反波函数且能量低于 YSR 状态的类似 Fulde-Ferrell-Larkin-Ovchinnikov (FFLO) 状态。来自 FFLO-like 状态的负面影响还导致了 ULRM 势内隙函数的符号变化。取决于产生 ULRM 势的 Rydberg 状态，向人口失衡的转变可能在 BCS 或 Bose-Einstein 凝聚态一侧发生。此外，自旋偏极化束缚态伴随着振荡“clumpy states”出现，以补偿局部密度差异。最后，我们讨论了复合 Rydberg 原子-费米超流体系统的可能实验实现和测量。
                                    https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.09836
