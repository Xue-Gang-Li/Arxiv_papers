    Kronecker states: a powerful source of multipartite maximally entangled states in quantum information

    Walther Gonzalez
    摘要：在量子信息理论中，极大纠缠态，特别是局部极大纠缠态（LME）态，对于量子协议至关重要。尽管许多研究集中在二分纠缠上，但量子误差校正和多方秘密共享等应用依赖于多体纠缠。这些LME态自然出现在对称群$S_n$的不可约表示的张量积的不变子空间中，即所谓的Kronecker子空间，其维度由Kronecker系数决定。一个Kronecker子空间是一个多方极大纠缠态的空间，它将高维Hilbert空间彼此纠缠。尽管这些态可以从$S_n$的Clebsch-Gordan系数中推导，但已知的方法即使对于小$n$也是低效的。一个基于量子信息的替代方法来自于纠缠浓缩协议，其中Kronecker子空间在多个纠缠态副本的等型分解中出现。已经为多qubit的W类态找到闭式形式，但一般情况尚无解决方案。本论文将这种方法扩展到任意多qubit系统。我们首先提出了一种称为W态拼接的图形构造，其中多qubit纠缠态表示为由W态构建的张量网络。通过分析这些图态副本的等型分解，可以构建相应的图Kronecker态。特别是，通用多qubit系统的图态可以生成任何Kronecker子空间。我们明确构造了三qubit和四qubit系统的基，并展示了W拼接技术也作为多qubit纠缠分类的有价值工具。这些结果可能在多体纠缠资源理论中开辟新的研究方向，以二分和三分W态为基础元素，并基于Kronecker态进行渐近分析。

            Keyi Yin, Xiang Fang, Zhuo Chen, Ang Li, David Hayes, Eneet Kaur, Reza Nejabati, Hartmut Haeffner, Wes Campbell, Eric Hudson, Jens Palsberg, Travis Humble, Yufei Ding
            摘要：Recent advances in quantum hardware and quantum error correction (QEC) have set the stage for early demonstrations of fault-tolerant quantum computing (FTQC). In this work, we propose a novel system architecture targeting MQC on trapped-ion quantum computers (TIQC), leveraging their ultra-high-fidelity single-qubit gates (1Q) and efficient two-qubit (2Q) logical CNOT gates enabled by the quantum charge-coupled device (QCCD) architecture with the ion shuttling feature. This approach avoids fully encoding all qubits, eliminating the overhead of gate synthesis, teleportation, and magic state distillation for non-Clifford gates. To support this, we design (1) a low-noise conversion protocol between bare and logical qubits, (2) a bare-logical hybrid instruction set architecture tailored for 2D grid-based TIQC, and (3) a compiler that minimizes conversion cost and optimizes the scheduling efficiency. We evaluate our approach on VQA and small-scale FTQC benchmarks, showing that it achieves superior performance improvements with significantly reduced resource overhead, offering a practical path toward early FTQC on TIQC.
