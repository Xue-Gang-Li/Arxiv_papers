                                                                    摘要：超导材料与系统（SQMS）中心，作为美国能源部的国家量子信息科学研究中心，开展了一项全面而有协调性的研究，利用具有已知性能指标的超导Transmon量子比特芯片，以识别设备间性能差异的根本材料来源。在量子比特相干性测量之后，这些基底超导金属和基底材料不同的量子比特在西北大学、阿姆斯国家实验室和费米实验室等地，通过盲测的方式，采用多种非破坏性和侵入性的材料表征技术进行了检查。我们发现，蚀刻基底沟槽深度、表面氧化物厚度以及侧壁几何形状的变化趋势，这些因素相互结合后，与不同设备中的T$_1$寿命存在相关性。此外，我们还列出了一系列设备间不同的特征，这些特征对性能的影响需要进一步研究。最后，我们确定了两种低温表征技术，可能作为量子比特测量的代理工具。这些见解不仅为减少邻近设备间性能差异提供了材料导向的解决方案，还可以通过工程设计和制造具有最优几何形状的设备，以实现超越现有最高水平的性能指标。

                                                                      Quantum dynamics of semiconductor quantum dot Josephson junctions

                                                                      Utkan Güngördü, Rusko Ruskov, Silas Hoffman, Kyle Serniak, Andrew J. Kerman, Charles Tahan
                                                                      摘要：This work presents a theoretical study of Josephson junctions made from superconductor-semiconductor-superconductor heterostructures, which are used to realize various voltage-tunable superconducting quantum devices such as qubits and parametric amplifiers. Previous theoretical descriptions were limited to small quantum fluctuations of the junction phase, but this work employs a path-integral formulation to self-consistently derive the phase quantum dynamics from an underlying many-body formalism. The method recovers known results for small phase fluctuations and predicts new effects: (i) system capacitances undergo gate-voltage-dependent renormalization; and (ii) an additional charge offset appears for asymmetric junctions. These results can be summarized in terms of a single-particle Hamiltonian, which can be directly compared to that of an ordinary Josephson junction. This more general theory could lead to the design of new quantum devices that go beyond voltage-tunable variants of previously-known circuits.
