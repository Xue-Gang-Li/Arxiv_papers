      Parametric Amplification in Kerr Nonlinear Resonators: A theoretical review of Josephson Parametric Amplifiers

      Rajlaxmi Bhoite, Shraddhanjali Choudhury
      摘要：This paper presents a detailed theoretical review of the amplification process in Josephson Parametric Amplifiers (JPAs), which are crucial for quantum-limited signal amplification in superconducting circuits. The paper begins by outlining the principles of parametric amplification, focusing on how a strong classical pump interacts with the nonlinear Josephson medium in reflection geometry. The key dynamical equations are derived under intense pumping, leading to a nonlinear steady-state solution. Linearization around this solution allows to analyze the system response to weak signals and extract expressions for parametric gain and intermodulation gain using the input-output formalism. Numerically, the equations are solved to explore how the gain depends on frequency detuning and pump strength, which is visualized with a gain response curve. By enhancing our understanding of JPAs, this work aims to inspire continued research in the field of quantum-limited amplification.

                                        摘要：我们理论上探索了一种基于对称式线圈的超导量子干涉器件（SQUID）的Kerr-cat量子比特替代电路。该对称式线圈超导量子干涉器件（STS）架构采用了简化的通磁泵设计，通过工程化驱动哈密顿量中的通磁量操作符仅生成偶数阶谐波，从而抑制高Kerr系数状态下的主导能量损耗机制——二光子耗散。STS在满足两项关键实用Kerr-cat量子比特运行标准方面表现出色：(1)具有稀释的Kerr非线性静态哈密顿量（通过STS的中间支实现）和(2)仅限于偶数阶谐波的驱动哈密顿量，确保了强健的二光子驱动并降低耗散。在弱Kerr非线性条件下，我们发现STS与SNAIL电路的相干态寿命（$T_\alpha$）相似。然而，STS Kerr-cat量子比特表现出对更高阶光子耗散的增强抵抗力，即使在较强的Kerr非线性（约10 MHz）下，也能显著延长$T_\alpha$。与SNAIL电路不同，在弱二光子驱动和高Kerr系数条件下，STS Kerr-cat量子比特显示出$T_\alpha$的低谷。我们证明，通过施加依赖驱动的偏移，可以抑制这种低谷，使得仅需八个约瑟夫森结（能量约80 GHz）即可实现Kerr-cat量子比特操作；对于更高能量的结，所需结数更少。此外，我们通过研究强通磁驱动和不对称约瑟夫森结对$T_\alpha$的影响来进一步验证STS设计的稳健性。在提出的设计中，并考虑到10个光子的猫尺寸，我们预测即使在多光子加热和去相位效应存在的情况下，$T_\alpha$也能达到数十毫秒级别。
