      Breakdown of Fermi's Golden Rule in 1d systems at non-zero temperature

      Thomas Young, Jerome LLoyd, Curt von Keyserlingk
      摘要：在相互作用的量子系统中，单粒子的Green函数预计会由于拟粒子的去相干效应而随时间衰减。在弱相互作用强度（$\Delta$）的极限情况下，Fermi黄金规则的简单应用预测了一个与$\mathcal{O}(\Delta^{2})$成正比的拟粒子衰变率。然而，对于一维格点上的发射体系统，当温度高于零度时，这种计算会导致结果发散，因此拟粒子的寿命随相互作用强度的缩放仍然是一个未解的问题。在这项工作中，我们提出了一个解决方案：结合最近引入的消ipation-assisted operator evolution（DAOE）方法的数值模拟和非扰动图解重叠加，我们预测了一个对数增强的拟粒子衰变率$\tau^{-1} \sim \Delta^{2} \log \Delta^{-2}$。我们认为这种效应存在于许多众所周知的弱相互作用量子发射体和玻色系统中，甚至在一些经典系统中，只要非相互作用极限有具有通用色散的拟粒子。

            Replacement-Type Quantum Gates

            Florian Ginzel, Javad Kazemi, Valentin Torggler, Wolfgang Lechner
            摘要：We introduce the paradigm of replacement-type quantum gates. This type of gate introduces input qubits, candidate qubits, and output qubits. The candidate qubits are prepared such, that a displacement conditional on the input qubit results in the targeted output state. Finally, the circuit continues with the output qubits constructed from the candidate qubits instead of the input qubits, thus the name "replacement-type gate". We present examples of replacement-type $X$ and $\mathrm{CNOT}$ gates realized with spin qubits and with neutral atom qubits. By making use of the extended Hilbert space, including the position of the particles, these gates approximately preserve the innate noise bias of the qubits. The gate preserves the noise-bias which motivates advanced quantum computer architectures with error correction.

              Stefan Häussler, Peter van Loock
              摘要：There are various approaches to long-range quantum communication based on conceptually different forms of quantum repeaters. Here we explore a quantum repeater scheme that employs quantum error correction (QEC) both on the flying (light) qubits and on the stationary (matter) qubits. While the encoded one-way and two-way schemes on their own either require very high repeater link coupling efficiencies and GKP squeezing or allow for experimentally more feasible, small values of these parameters, respectively, we find that there are intermediate parameter regimes where the combined repeater protocol is superior

                                      Celestial Quantum Error Correction I: Qubits from Noncommutative Klein Space

                                      Alfredo Guevara, Yangrui Hu
                                      摘要：在4D渐近平坦时空中，量子重力由于软辐射毛发而具有自发的对称性破坏，与红外分歧的增殖密切相关。通过一个假设的2D共形场理论（CFT）的全息描述预计没有这种冗余。在这两部分论文中，我们通过启动天体共形场理论（CCFT）中的量子错误校正研究来解决这个问题。在第一部分中，我们通过重新审视Kleinian双曲凯勒空间的非交换几何，构造了一个有限度数的玩具模型。该模型服从在径向方向归一化的Wick代数，并允许Gottesman-Kitaev-Preskill风格的等距嵌入。代码子空间由2-qubit稳定态组成，这些稳定态在软时空波动下强健。双曲凯勒空间的对称性变为离散的，并转化为熟悉的量子计算中的Clifford群。然后，该构造被嵌入到扭子空间的发生关系中，为即将进行的CCFT政权铺平道路。
