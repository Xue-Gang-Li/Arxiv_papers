On the hardness of approximating minimum distances of quantum codes

Elena Grigorescu, Vatsal Jha, Eric Samperton
摘要：This paper discusses the computational hardness of determining distances for error-correcting codes in both classical and quantum contexts. It reproves the quantum setting's difficulty using hypergraph product codes, focusing on CSS codes, a common type of quantum code. Additionally, it addresses the NP-hardness of computing graph state distances, particularly under X-type errors, with implications for classical codes with specific rates.
