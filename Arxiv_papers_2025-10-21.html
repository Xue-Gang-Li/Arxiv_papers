      Coherence-Mediated Quantum Thermometry in a Hybrid Circuit-QED Architecture

      Shaojiang Zhu, Xinyuan You, Alexander Romanenko, Anna Grassellino
      摘要：This work proposes and theoretically analyzes a hybrid circuit quantum electrodynamics architecture, where a superconducting qubit is dispersively coupled to two distinct bosonic modes: one initialized in a weak coherent state and the other coupled to a thermal environment. The qubit serves as a sensitive readout of the probe mode, mapping the interference between thermal and coherent photon-number fluctuations onto measurable dephasing. This mechanism enables enhanced sensitivity to sub-millikelvin thermal energy fluctuations through Ramsey interferometry. Analytic expressions for the qubit coherence envelope are derived, quantum Fisher information for temperature estimation is computed, and numerical demonstration shows that a coherent reference amplifies the qubit's sensitivity to small changes in thermal photon occupancy. These results establish a new paradigm for quantum-enhanced thermometry and provide a scalable platform for future calorimetric sensing in high-energy physics and quantum metrology
