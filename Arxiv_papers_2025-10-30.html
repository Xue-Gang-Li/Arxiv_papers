          Decoherence Estimation of Superconducting Qubit

          Yoav Koral, Shilo Avraham, Manimuthu Peryasamy, Shmuel E. Schacham, Eliyahu Farber
          摘要：Decoherence of quantum bits arises primarily from the parasitic resistance within the qubit. This study presents the analysis of the decoherence process due to physical interactions between the qubit photons and parasitic resistance atoms, utilizing exclusively the Caldeira-Leggett electrical model, without relying on external Hamiltonians. The analysis shows a good agreement between the model of the electrical noise and the Johnson-Nyquist noise. The emission and absorption rates of the qubit's coherent loss, required for the Lindblad master equation that approximates the decoherence, are obtained. A numerical substitution in the analysis result yields a strong correlation with previous measurements. The present analysis enables also the derivation of the appropriate circuit characteristics for future simulations.

                    Accurate Leakage Speculation for Quantum Error Correction

                    Chaithanya Naik Mude, Swamit Tannu
                    摘要：这段文字讨论了量子错误校正（QEC）中的漏出问题，其中物理量子比特可能会泄漏到更高的能级，如|2>状态。这种泄漏会导致后续综合征测量受损，并可能扩散到邻近的量子比特。在数据量子比特上检测泄漏尤为困难，因为它们在QEC周期中不会被直接测量。先前的工作如“擦除器”通过固定启发式从综合征模式推断泄漏，但这种方法常常误判良性综合征，触发过多的泄漏减少电路（LRCs）。由于这些LRCs本身噪声大且速度慢，这些错误触发延长了QEC周期，并增加了逻辑错误率。作者提出了Gladiator，一种通用适应性强的泄漏猜测框架，能够在表面代码、颜色代码和qLDPC编码中工作。在离线时，Gladiator根据设备数据构建校正码感知的错误传播图。在线时，它对每个综合征进行分类，只需几纳秒，便可确定是否需要安排LRC。这精确的猜测消除了多达3倍（平均2倍）不必要的LRCs，缩短了QEC周期，并从根本上抑制了误报。在标准容错benchmark中评估，Gladiator带来1.7x-3.9x速度提升和16%逻辑错误率降低，提高了容错量子计算效率。
